•A collection of domain-specific and domain-independent tools combined to “aspectize” the distributed character of server-side applications.
•A framework to turn objects of a large class of unaware applications into distributed objects with minimal programming effort.
•Constitutes a significant improvement over past efforts to isolate distribution concerns.
GOTECH is built on top of three main components:
•AspectJ a high-level aspect language,
•XDoclet a low-level aspect language,
•NRMI (Natural RMI) a middleware facility that                     makes remote calls behave more like                    local calls.
