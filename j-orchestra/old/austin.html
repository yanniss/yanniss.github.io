Research

I have developed mostly bytecode engineering and rewriting tools with the BCEL library, for things like adding mutators/accessors for class on bytecode level, renaming field references, etc. I also built another tool to generate a snapshot callgraph of how classes communicate in an application statically from their bytecodes. I am also in charged of the GUI frontend of j-Orchestra, except the visual representation of callgraph which is done by Dean.
